# Technical Manager for Top Dutch Solar Racing

As Technical Manager, you coordinate the full technical program: planning each phase, aligning subgroups, managing risks and budgets, and ensuring deliverables meet regulations and deadlines. You work hand-in-hand with the Chief Engineer so designs are integrated, documented, and ready for scrutineering, testing, and race operations.

More info at https://www.solarracing.nl

## Highlights

- Phase planning & critical path
- Cross-team syncs & risk management
- Docs, regulations & scrutineering

## What you will do

- Plan each project phase and major deadlines, together with the Chief Engineer.

- Run weekly meetings with the Chief Engineer and each subgroup (Electrical, Mechanical, Aero, Structural, Software, Strategy) to review progress, blockers, improvements and risks.

- Review technical budgets and give green light for major technical orders.

- Master the challenge regulations; translate them into clear, testable requirements and checks, andn eventually know them by heart.

- Ensure all technical documentation is written, reviewed, and submitted on time to the organization.

- Lead static scrutineering preparation and execution; define evidence packs, procedures, and acceptance criteria.

- Book and coordinate testing time early; secure facilities, permits, staffing, and logistics.

- Establish change-control for late design updates; protect schedule and integration stability.

- Coordinate build-readiness reviews, tooling/spares lists, and on-vehicle validation plans to ensure smooth assembly and track readiness.

## Are you...

- Someone that thrives under pressure, can handle setbacks, and adapts quickly to new challenges?

- A person that communicates clearly and manages time effectively?

- An active listener who can connect information from different sources?

- Someone that already has technical knowledge and has the curiosity and capability to quickly grasp the basics of every engineering subgroup?
